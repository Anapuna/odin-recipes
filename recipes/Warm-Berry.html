Warm Berry Compote

Description

This berry compote is made in a slow cooker with frozen berries and orange juice. It's a yummy dessert by itself or over ice cream.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups frozen mixed berries
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ¼ cup orange juice
  • 1½ teaspoons finely grated orange zest
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water

Steps

  1. Stir frozen berries, sugar, orange juice, and orange zest together in a slow cooker.
  2. Cover and cook on High until bubbling, about 1½ hours.
  3. Stir cornstarch and water together in a cup until fully dissolved. Stir into berry mixture.
  4. Cover again and cook until thickened, 5 to 10 minutes.
  5. Serve warm or at room temperature.
